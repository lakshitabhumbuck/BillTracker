Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a new bill?

Go to the Bills page and click "Add Bill". Fill in the bill details including name, amount, due date, and category. Click "Save" to add it to your bills list.

How do I add a credit card?

Navigate to Credit Cards → Add Card. Enter your card details including company, number, holder name, expiry date, and CVV. The card will be securely stored and you can view it in Manage Cards.

How do I view my spending insights?

Go to Credit Cards → Smart Insights. You can view spending patterns by category and vendor, with interactive charts. Use the month/year filter to see data for specific periods.

How do I check my credit card statements?

Visit Credit Cards → View Statements. Select the year and month, then click "Get Statement" to see all transactions for that period.

Is my financial data secure?

Yes, we use industry-standard encryption to protect your data. All sensitive information is encrypted and stored securely. We never share your personal or financial information with third parties.

How do I delete a bill or credit card?

For bills: Go to the Bills page, find the bill you want to delete, and click the "Delete" button. For credit cards: Go to Credit Cards → Manage Cards and click "Delete" on the card you want to remove.
