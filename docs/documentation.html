📘 Overview

Welcome to the official documentation of Credify. This comprehensive personal finance management app helps you track bills, manage credit cards, analyze spending patterns, and stay on top of your financial health. This guide will help you understand all features, how to use them effectively, and best practices to get the most out of the app.

🎯 Key Benefits:

  • Track and manage all your bills in one place
  • Secure credit card management with transaction history
  • Smart spending insights with interactive charts
  • AI-powered assistant for instant help
  • Responsive design works on all devices

🔧 Features Guide

1. 📋 Bills Management

Purpose: Track and manage all your recurring bills and payments.

How to Use:

  • Add Bills: Click "Add Bill" to enter bill details (name, amount, due date, category)
  • View Bills: See all bills in an organized table with status indicators
  • Filter: Filter by status (Paid, Unpaid, Upcoming) or view in calendar format
  • Edit/Delete: Modify or remove bills as needed
💡 Tip: Use the calendar view to see your payment schedule at a glance.

2. 💳 Credit Cards

Purpose: Manage multiple credit cards and track transactions.

How to Use:

  • Add Cards: Securely store card information with 3D preview
  • Manage Cards: View, edit, or delete stored cards
  • View Statements: Check transaction history by month/year
  • Smart Insights: Analyze spending patterns with interactive charts
💡 Tip: The 3D card preview shows your card details in real-time as you type.

3. 📊 Smart Insights

Purpose: Get detailed analytics and spending breakdowns.

How to Use:

  • Monthly Analysis: View spending by category and vendor
  • Interactive Charts: Pie charts and bar graphs for visual analysis
  • Filter by Period: Select specific months to analyze spending patterns
  • Trends: Track spending habits over time
💡 Tip: Use the month/year filter to compare spending across different periods.

4. 🤖 AI Assistant

Purpose: Get instant help and answers to common questions.

How to Use:

  • Chat Interface: Natural conversation with the AI assistant
  • Quick Actions: Get bills info, credit card details, payment help
  • Smart Responses: Context-aware answers based on your data
  • Navigation Help: Get guided to the right pages and features
💡 Tip: Use quick reply buttons for faster interaction with the AI assistant.

5. 🧭 Navigation

Purpose: Easy access to all app features and sections.

How to Use:

  • Top Menu: Quick access to Dashboard, Bills, Credit Cards, Insights
  • Breadcrumbs: Know exactly where you are in the app
  • Back Navigation: Easy return to previous pages
  • Responsive Design: Works seamlessly on desktop and mobile
💡 Tip: Breadcrumbs help you navigate back to previous pages easily.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I add a new bill?
A: Go to the Bills page, click "Add Bill", fill in the details (name, amount, due date, category), and click "Save".
Q: Can I edit or delete bills?
A: Yes, on the Bills page, each bill has "Edit" and "Delete" buttons for easy management.
Q: How do I add a credit card?
A: Navigate to Credit Cards → Add Card, enter your card details, and click "Add Card". Your information is securely stored.
Q: Is my financial data secure?
A: Yes, we use industry-standard encryption and secure database practices. Your sensitive information is never shared.
Q: How do I view my spending insights?
A: Go to Credit Cards → Smart Insights, select a month/year, and view interactive charts showing your spending patterns.
Q: Can I export my data?
A: Currently, data export features are in development. Your data is always accessible through the app interface.
Q: How do I use the AI Assistant?
A: Go to Help & Support page, click "Start Chat", and ask questions about bills, cards, payments, or general help.
Q: What payment methods are supported?
A: The app supports UPI, Credit/Debit Cards, Net Banking, and NEFT/RTGS for bill payments.

🛠 Technical Information

Technology Stack

  • Frontend: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript (Vanilla)
  • Backend: Node.js, Express.js
  • Database: PostgreSQL
  • Charts: Chart.js for data visualization
  • AI: Rule-based chatbot with natural language processing

System Requirements

  • Browser Support: Modern browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
  • Mobile: Responsive design works on all mobile devices
  • Internet: Requires internet connection for real-time data
  • Storage: No local storage required, all data is cloud-based
